Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2025

Happy Golf LLC ("Happy," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website joinhappy.golf and the Happy Golf mobile application (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Identity data: your full name
  • Contact data: email address and phone number
  • Profile data: handicap index, pace of play preference, home course, industry, and occupation
  • Usage data: how you interact with the Service, rounds hosted or joined, and activity within the app
  • Communications: any messages or feedback you send us

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Review your membership application and operate the Service
  • Create and manage your account
  • Match you with compatible golfers and rounds
  • Send transactional emails (application status, round confirmations, join request notifications)
  • Send promotional and marketing communications if you have consented — you may opt out at any time
  • Improve, personalize, and develop the Service
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Marketing Communications

If you check the marketing consent box on our application form, we may send you emails about membership updates, new features, curated round recommendations, and other Happy-related news. Every marketing email includes an unsubscribe link. You can opt out at any time by:

  • Clicking "Unsubscribe" in any marketing email
  • Emailing us at hello@joinhappy.golf

Opting out of marketing emails will not affect transactional service emails related to your account.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers: third parties who assist us in operating the Service (e.g., Supabase for database infrastructure, Apple for authentication). These parties are bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Other members: certain profile information (name, handicap, pace preference, photo) is visible to other Happy members as part of the round-matching experience.
  • Legal requirements: if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of Happy and its members.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at hello@joinhappy.golf.

6. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, object to or restrict certain processing, or withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@joinhappy.golf.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance.
