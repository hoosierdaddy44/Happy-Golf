Your Circle
Play with your crew.
Meet your next one.

Bring your friends

Already golf with a crew? Create a private group, add them, and keep all your rounds in one place. Your circle, your rules.

🔒
Post rounds exclusively to your group

Keep certain rounds off the public feed. Post to your group only — so the right people see the right rounds.

🌿
Find new players too

Browse open rounds from vetted members near you. Request a spot, get approved, and expand your network one great round at a time.

🚫
No randoms — even within your network

Every group is curated. Even when you expand your circle, every new face is someone you chose to let in.

After Your Round
The round doesn't end
on 18.
📊
Round Summary & Leaderboard

Every round gets a net leaderboard. See who won, net scores vs. par, and the full player breakdown — all in one view.

✅
Score Verification

Co-players verify each other's scores after the round. No self-reporting. No disputes. Everyone in the group signs off.

🏆
Claim Accolades

Eagle? Broke 80? Hole-in-one? Log your accolades after the round and get them verified by your playing partners.

⭐
Rate Your Playing Partners

Every round ends with a mutual rating. Good players build a reputation that unlocks better rounds and tighter groups.
